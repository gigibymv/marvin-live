I. Thesis

The classical consulting pyramid was built for a world without agents. We have redrawn it.

01 / Leverage

One partner, one hundred analysts, one morning.

Work that once required a bullpen of twenty for three weeks now completes overnight. Our agents run the reading, the modeling, the first drafts. Humans judge, argue, and sign.

02 / Continuity

The engagement never sleeps.

Between your meetings, agents revisit assumptions, refresh market data, stress-test scenarios, and flag what changed. You return to a deck that aged a day and grew sharper for it.

03 / Accountability

Every claim, every source, every step.

Every figure in our deliverables carries a signed trail back to the agent that computed it and the human who approved it. No black boxes. Deliverables are replayable.

VI. Principles of the house

Six rules we will not trade away.

i.

A human signs every page.

No deliverable leaves the firm without a named partner attesting to it. Agents draft; humans are accountable.

ii.

Uncertainty is a first-class citizen.

We report confidence intervals, not point estimates. When we do not know, we say so, and we tell you what it would cost to find out.

iii.

Your data stays your data.

Agents run inside isolated enclaves you can audit. Nothing you share trains a model outside your walls. Ever.

iv.

We price the answer, not the hour.

Fixed fees per question. When agents make us faster, you pay less. When we need humans to stay longer, we absorb it.

v.

The deliverable is replayable.

Every engagement ships with a console. You can re-run our analysis on new data, six months later, without us.

vi.

We leave you more capable.

A successful engagement ends with agents operating inside your team, not ours. We teach you to run them.

vii.

Small rooms, short memos.

Four humans, nine agents, one page. We refuse the theater of the fifty-slide deck. A decision deserves a memo, not a performance.

viii.

The partner takes the call.

No gatekeepers, no assistants, no queues. The partner who sold the work does the work, and answers the phone at 10pm.
